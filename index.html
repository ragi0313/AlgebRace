How to Play

Roll the Dice to determine how far you move

You may land on the following colored tiles:

Blue Tile

Green Tile

Red Tile

Blue Tile

A random CHANCE card will pop up when your chosen race piece landed on the blue/chance tile. The card contains AIR POLLUTION preventive measures with consecutive advantages.

Green Tile

A random CHARGE card will pop up when your chosen race piece landed on the green/charge tile. The card contains AIR POLLUTION causes with consecutive disadvantages.

Red Tile

A random Algebraic question will pop up from the "Oh no! It's Math Time" deck. You will have to answer this to advance. Failure to answer the question correctly will result to a consequence indicated in the card.
